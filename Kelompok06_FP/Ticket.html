Ticket Sales Information

Tickets for matches at the Etihad and Academy Stadiums can be purchased via the following methods:

Online

Tickets for all matches are available to purchase online at mancity.com/tickets.

Tickets purchased on line will be subject to a £1.50 booking fee per ticket, excluding Seasoncard holders & Cityzens Matchday members.

Please note: For tickets purchased online, supporters will be allocated a Print@Home ticket and are reminded to print their ticket(s) on white A4 plain paper prior to leaving for the match.

Read our Print@Home guide.

Phone

Tickets for men’s first team matches only can be booked by phone on +(0)161 444 1894 (Option 1, Option 1, Option 2).

Tickets purchased over the telephone will be subject to a £2.50 booking fee per ticket and phone lines are open 24-hours, seven days a week.

In Person

Tickets for all matches are available from the Ticket Office above the City Store at the Etihad Stadium.

The Ticket Office and Supporter Services are open Monday to Friday, 9am until 5.30pm (closed Weekends and Bank Holidays).

Both facilities will open on the day of all scheduled First Team home fixtures from until 15 minutes after kick-off.

You can contact Supporter Services on +44 (0)161 444 1894 (Option 1, Option 3).

The Supporter Services phone line opening hours are Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm. The Ticket Office opening times are Monday to Friday, 9am to 5.30pm. Please note, on Thursday both Supporter Services and the Ticket Office will open from 10am (closed Weekends and Bank Holidays).

All tickets are sold subject to availability and are issued subject to Manchester City Footballs Club’s Home Match Terms and Conditions of Sale and the stadium’s Ground Regulations.

Keep up to date with matchday news via our official service Twitter account @MANCITYHELP.

Accessible tickets

Qualifying disabled supporters are able to purchase match tickets in the following ways:

Online at mancity.com/tickets *

• Call the Access Team on +44 (0)161 444 1894 (Option 1,1,1)

• In person at the Etihad Stadium Ticket Office located on the first floor of the City Store

Further information regarding accessibility, including the Club’s Access Statement, can be found here.
